# Partnership Proposal: Memory Forward × Estate Sale Companies

**Prepared by:** Memory Forward
**Date:** April 3, 2026

---

## A Resource for Your Most Discerning Clients

Estate sales for high-value estates often surface something no auctioneer can price: decades of family photographs, 8mm home films, hand-labeled slide carousels, film negatives. These aren't items to be sold. They're the part of the estate families worry about most — and the part they're least equipped to handle.

Memory Forward exists for exactly this moment.

---

## What We Do

We provide a white glove media preservation service for families with irreplaceable physical archives. Our work spans printed photographs, film negatives, slides, VHS and Beta tapes, and 8mm film — handled with the same level of care the family would bring themselves, if they had the expertise.

Every engagement includes:

- **Personal consultation** to understand the scope and significance of the collection
- **In-home or on-site pickup** — media never gets dropped in a shipping box
- **Archival-grade digitization** by trained specialists, not automated batch processing
- **A curated digital archive** — organized, labeled, and delivered in a format the family will actually use for generations
- **White-glove return** of all original media, in the same condition we received it

We work slowly and deliberately. We take on a limited number of clients at a time. That is by design.

---

## Why We're Reaching Out to You

Estate sale professionals at the top of the market encounter families navigating complex, emotionally loaded transitions. You've built your reputation on discretion, expertise, and knowing the right people to call.

We'd like to be one of those people.

We're not looking for volume referrals. We're looking for a small number of trusted partners who encounter clients with significant collections and want to offer them something genuinely valuable — a resource they wouldn't easily find on their own.

---

## What a Referral Looks Like

There's no program to sign up for. When you encounter a family with a meaningful media archive and you think they'd benefit from speaking with us, you make an introduction. We take it from there, and we represent you well.

If it's helpful, we're happy to provide materials you can share with clients, or to make ourselves available for a brief conversation whenever the situation arises.

---

## About Memory Forward

We are a local, independently owned studio. Our clients tend to be families for whom cost is secondary to trust — people who want to know that the only photographs of their grandmother's childhood are in careful hands.

We'd welcome the chance to meet in person, show you our process, and let you decide whether we're the right fit for your clients.
