# New Employee Onboarding Guide

Welcome to Memory Forward! This guide will walk you through your first week and make sure you're
set up to do great work.

---

## Day 1 — Getting Oriented

### Accounts to Set Up
- [ ] Company email (contact IT: it@memoryforward.com)
- [ ] Slack workspace (`memoryforward.slack.com`)
- [ ] GitHub access — ask your team lead to add you to the `memory-forward` org
- [ ] Customer portal login (internal URL shared in Slack `#welcome`)
- [ ] Shared network drive access (`\\mfshare\jobs`)

### Physical Workspace
- Locate your assigned scanning station (labeled with your name by your team lead)
- Familiarize yourself with the equipment at your station:
  - Flatbed scanner (Epson Perfection V850 or V600)
  - Film/slide attachment tray
  - Anti-static gloves (use these whenever handling negatives or slides)
  - Cotton lint-free cloths
  - Job tray inbox/outbox

---

## Day 2 — Core Processes

Read through the following documents before handling any customer orders:

1. [Standard Order Workflow](../workflows/standard-order-workflow.md)
2. [File Naming Conventions](../standards/file-naming-conventions.md)
3. [Quality Standards](../standards/quality-standards.md)

### Shadow a Senior Technician
Spend at least half of Day 2 watching an experienced technician process a full order from intake
through delivery. Ask questions freely — there are no dumb questions here.

---

## Day 3–5 — Supervised Practice

- Process 2–3 real orders under supervision
- Your team lead will review your output before it goes to the customer
- Focus on: scan quality, file organization, and accurate metadata entry

## Important Policies

- **Never** discard or return customer media without explicit sign-off from the Operations Lead
- All customer data is confidential — do not share files outside approved channels
- If you damage a customer item, report it to Jamie immediately — accidents happen, cover-ups don't
- Work-in-progress orders must be stored in labeled trays in the secure cabinet at end of day

---

## After Your First Week

Once you've completed onboarding, you'll be assigned your own job queue. Check in with your team
lead each morning for your day's assignments via the job board in Slack `#daily-jobs`.
